Colophon

Discourses on Livy
was published in 1517 by
Niccolò Machiavelli.
It was translated from Italian in 1883 by
Ninian Hill Thomson.

This ebook was produced for
Standard Ebooks
by
An Anonymous Volunteer,
and is based on a transcription produced in 2004 by
Ted Garvin, Jayam Subramanian, and Distributed Proofreaders
for
Project Gutenberg
and on digital scans from the
Internet Archive.

The cover page is adapted from
Brutus Condemning His Sons to Death,
a painting completed in 1811 by
Guillaume Guillon Lethière.
The cover and title pages feature the
League Spartan and Sorts Mill Goudy
typefaces created in 2014 and 2009 by
The League of Moveable Type.
